## Get in touch

We're here to help! Choose the best way to reach us based on your needs.

### Community support

Join our community for quick answers and discussions:

- **Discord** - Chat with our team and community members in real-time
- **GitHub Discussions** - Ask questions and share ideas with other developers

### Email support

For account-specific issues or private inquiries:

**support@example.com**

We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

### Enterprise support

Enterprise customers have access to:

- Dedicated support channels
- Priority response times
- Direct access to engineering team
- Custom SLA agreements

Contact your account manager or email **enterprise@example.com** for more information.

### Report a bug

Found a bug? Help us improve by reporting it:

1. Check existing issues on [GitHub](https://github.com/mintlify)
2. Create a new issue with detailed reproduction steps
3. Include relevant error messages and logs

### Documentation feedback

Notice something wrong or missing in our docs?

- Submit a pull request on GitHub
- Use the feedback widget at the bottom of any page
- Email us at **docs@example.com**

## Response times

| Support Type | Response Time |
|--------------|---------------|
| Community (Discord/GitHub) | Best effort |
| Email support | 24-48 hours |
| Enterprise support | 4-8 hours |
| Critical issues (Enterprise) | 1-2 hours |

## Before contacting support

To help us assist you faster, please have the following ready:

- Your account email or organization name
- Detailed description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Any relevant error messages or logs
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
